CLAIMS PROCESSING TIME REDUCTION AND WORKFLOW AUTOMATION

BrightSecure Insurance Ltd., a leading UK-based insurer, faced growing customer dissatisfaction due to lengthy, manual claims processing taking up to 14 days. This inefficiency led to increased operational costs, compliance risks, and a high volume of support calls. The task was to identify root causes, map the current state, propose an improved future-state process, and recommend automation solutions that reduce claim turnaround time and improve customer satisfaction—while ensuring regulatory compliance. I conducted stakeholder interviews, developed AS-IS and TO-BE process maps, performed 5 Whys and fishbone root cause analysis, and identified key pain points, including manual document verification, lack of system integration, and communication gaps. The proposed solution included the introduction of OCR for document intake, RPA bots for automation, and CRM/API tools for real-time status updates. KPIs were also defined to track success. The initiative projected a 65–70% reduction in claim turnaround time, a 50–70% increase in customer retention, and cost savings of up to £75,000 per year. Staff workload was expected to decrease significantly, with over 70% automation coverage, resulting in higher productivity, an improved customer experience, and stronger compliance assurance.

TeleCare Connect Expansion Project: Enhancing Access to Healthcare

TeleCare Connect, a global telemedicine leader, struggled to reach underserved areas due to poor connectivity, low awareness, and EHR integration issues. The aim is to expand access by improving platform reliability, usability, and compliance while addressing stakeholder pain points. I Designed As-Is/To-Be process maps, introduced a Lite Access Layer with low-bandwidth options, automated scheduling, and FHIR-compliant EHR integration. This led to 95% low-bandwidth session success, reduced missed appointments by 50%, improved patient satisfaction (≥4.5/5), and saved clinicians 5 minutes per consultation.
